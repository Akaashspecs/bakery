20 Amazing True Blue Australian Themed Logos to Celebrate Australia Day

Happy Australia Day everyone! Perhaps the greatest thing about the Aussie national holiday is there's something to love for everyone: You can celebrate by having a barbie in the sun with your family, hitting the waves or football field with your mates, joining Invasion Day events to commemorate the suffering of Aboriginals at the hands of early settlers, or kick back at home and just enjoy your day off work.

One of the most important parts of official Australia Day proceedings are the Australian of the Year Awards, honoring distinguished Aussies who have changed the nation for the better.

So to celebrate all that is great about DesignCrowd's home nation, we've decided to present our very own honor roll of Australian-inspired logos.

We sure didn't have to look far. Australian culture is just brimming with inspiring and inspired design, utilizing world-famous Aussie icons like the unique wildlife and the instantly recognizable boomerang. Our favorite aspect of this roundup though has to be the demonstrated versatility of the very shape of the nation, which is effortlessly morphed into the palm of a hand, the body of a sheep, and part of an energetic wave.
